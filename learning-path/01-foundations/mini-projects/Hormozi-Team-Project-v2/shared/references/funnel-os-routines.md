# Funnel OS Routines — INFUSE Methodology Reference

Systematic workflow for validating, structuring, and scaling business opportunities. Derived from the unified Hormozi + Russell Brunson + Valdo framework (Funnel OS 6.3). The six routines are applied in sequence; skipping earlier routines (especially Validation) before later ones is prohibited.

---

## Routine 1 — Validation

**Purpose:** Determine whether the idea has real market demand before investing in offers, funnels, or traffic.

**Key Steps:**
1. Summarize the user's idea in 1-2 sentences.
2. Apply the "Find Signals, Find Transactions, Test Interest" framework.
3. Deliver 3-5 key questions the idea must answer.
4. Propose 2-3 rapid tests (survey, waitlist, pre-offer) executable in 48h or less.
5. Gate: if validation is insufficient, do NOT recommend scaling ads or building complex funnels.

**Primary Knowledge:** `valdo_hormozi_product_validation_guide.md`
**Secondary Knowledge:** `hormozi_100m_offers.md` (market/niche selection), `hormozi_and_russell_teoria_unificada.md`

---

## Routine 2 — Transformation and Offer

**Purpose:** Design a Grand Slam offer anchored to a concrete transformation (Day 1 to Day 90).

**Key Steps:**
1. Define the Day 1 Avatar (current pains, context, situation).
2. Define the Day 90 Avatar (specific, observable result).
3. Formulate the promise: "A goes to B in Z days without X, by means of Y."
4. Apply the Value Equation — increase Dream Outcome and Perceived Likelihood; reduce Time Delay and Effort/Sacrifice.
5. Output: one Grand Transformative Promise + offer bullets (fewer pieces, more impact).

**Primary Knowledge:** `hormozi_100m_offers.md`, `hormozi_course_theory.md` (BECOME framework)
**Secondary Knowledge:** `hormozi_edukami_angle.md` (Day 1 to Day 90 framing), `hormozi_and_russell_teoria_unificada.md`

---

## Routine 3 — Narrative and Beliefs

**Purpose:** Build the persuasion layer that makes purchasing the offer feel obvious.

**Key Steps:**
1. Define the Big Domino: "If you believe [mechanism], then buying [offer] becomes obvious."
2. Classify as New Opportunity or Opportunity Switch.
3. Produce minimum deliverables: 1 tagline, 1 Big Promise one-liner, 3 mini-stories (Epiphany Bridges) breaking key false beliefs.
4. Optionally extend to: VSL/webinar script, landing page story section.

**Primary Knowledge:** `russell_clickfunnels_narrativepsycho.md` (Expert Secrets, Big Domino, Epiphany Bridge)
**Secondary Knowledge:** `hormozi_edukami_angle.md`, `hormozi_and_russell_teoria_unificada.md`

---

## Routine 4 — Funnel Architecture

**Purpose:** Map the offer onto the Value Ladder and select the right funnel type.

**Key Steps:**
1. Place the offer on the Value Ladder: Lead Magnet, Frontend, Core, or High Ticket.
2. Select funnel type by price/friction/trust: Lead Magnet/Squeeze, Tripwire/SLO, Webinar/VSL, Product Launch, or Application/High Ticket.
3. Define funnel steps (P1, P2, P3...) with objective per page.
4. Set baseline KPIs: opt-in rate, CTR, show-up rate, cost per lead, earnings per lead.
5. Output: text funnel schematic, measurement plan per step, 2-3 quick improvements if existing funnel is live.

**Primary Knowledge:** `russell_clickFunnels_dotcomSecrets_FunnelBuilding.md`, `russell_clickfunnels_leadFunnelsBook.md`
**Secondary Knowledge:** `hormozi_and_russell_teoria_unificada.md`

---

## Routine 5 — Traffic and Distribution

**Purpose:** Drive qualified attention to the funnel through focused channel selection.

**Key Steps:**
1. Select 1-2 organic channels and 1-2 paid channels (no more).
2. Enforce: one funnel = one avatar = one angle = one CTA. No mixing.
3. For each channel, produce: 3 hooks/angles, mini-structure (Hook, Story, Offer) for each, and optionally 1-2 DM or ad scripts.
4. Apply Core Four traffic sources and Dream 100 methodology where relevant.

**Primary Knowledge:** `hormozi_100m_leads.md`, `russell_clickfunnels_trafficBook.md`
**Secondary Knowledge:** `hormozi_and_russell_teoria_unificada.md`

---

## Routine 6 — Follow-up and Monetization

**Purpose:** Maximize lead and customer value through sequenced communication and ascension paths.

**Key Steps:**
1. Design a Soap Opera Sequence (5-7 emails) for new leads.
2. Design ongoing Seinfeld-style emails (content/entertainment, daily or frequent).
3. Define post-purchase upsell (immediate).
4. Define path for non-buyers (nurture/re-engage).
5. Define ascension route to High Ticket or next Value Ladder rung.
6. When "emails" or "sequence" is requested, always clarify which type: Soap Opera, Seinfeld, launch, etc.

**Primary Knowledge:** `russell_clickFunnels_dotcomSecrets_FunnelBuilding.md`, `russell_clickfunnels_leadFunnelsBook.md`
**Secondary Knowledge:** `russell_clickfunnels_narrativepsycho.md` (Expert Secrets email frameworks), `hormozi_and_russell_teoria_unificada.md`
